Welcome To UCSC E-Leave Forum


In this portal UCSC Acadamic and Non-Acadamic staff can apply thier leave request online. so that they wouldn't apply leaverequest manyally anymore.

Procedure

  • if you are member of UCSC Acadamic or Non Acadamic staff you can apply for e-leave facility
  • first Register to this facility via Acadamic office(through the system)
  • then you can make your own e-leave request!
  • you can manage your leave,leave history
  • also you can watch leave availble dates and not-availble dates
